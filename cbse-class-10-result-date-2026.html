CBSE Class 10 Result Date 2026 — What We Know

CBSE has not officially announced the Class 10 result date for 2026 as of March 2026. The official date will be published on cbse.gov.in a few days before results are declared. Based on the consistent pattern from the past four years, here is what you can expect:

Pattern: Excluding 2022 (which was delayed due to COVID disruptions), CBSE has declared Class 10 results on 12–13 May for three consecutive years. The 2025–26 board exams ran February–March 2026 on schedule, so a May 2026 result is expected.

How CBSE announces: CBSE typically sends a press release to media 1–2 days before results. You will see it as news on major websites. The official confirmation comes from cbse.gov.in.

What to Do Right Now — Before Results

Do not wait for result day to prepare. Here is what to do this week:

  • Set up DigiLocker — create your account, verify with Aadhaar, and link your CBSE roll number. Takes 10 minutes. Your marksheet will be ready the moment results are declared.
  • Keep your admit card safe — you need the roll number and school number to check your result on official portals.
  • Know your stream requirements — use the Stream Eligibility Calculator with your expected marks so you are not surprised on result day.
  • Understand the grading system — read our Grading System guide so you know what your A1, A2, B1 grades mean before you see them.
  • Know the re-evaluation deadlines — the window opens within days of results. If any paper went badly, be ready to apply. See our Re-evaluation guide.

Frequently Asked Questions

When will CBSE Class 10 result 2026 come? ▼

CBSE has not announced the official date yet. Based on the pattern from 2023, 2024, and 2025 — all of which saw results on 12–13 May at 2:00 PM — the most likely date for 2026 result is the second week of May 2026. The official announcement will be made on cbse.gov.in 1–2 days before results are declared.

Which website is fastest to check CBSE result? ▼

DigiLocker is consistently the fastest and most reliable on result day. It runs on separate government infrastructure from the main CBSE portals and handles traffic better. Set up your DigiLocker account now and link your roll number — your marksheet will appear there automatically when results come.

Will CBSE Class 10 result 2026 come before Class 12? ▼

Typically Class 12 results come first — usually in late April or early May — followed by Class 10 results a week or two later. In 2025, Class 12 results came on 13 May and Class 10 also on 13 May. The exact sequence for 2026 will be announced by CBSE closer to the date.

Can I check CBSE result without my roll number? ▼

No — the roll number is mandatory to check your result on official portals like cbseresults.nic.in. However, if you have set up DigiLocker with your Aadhaar and it is linked to your CBSE registration, your marksheet may appear there without needing to enter a roll number manually. Your roll number is printed on your CBSE admit card — keep it accessible.

What should I do immediately after checking my result? ▼

First, screenshot your result and download the DigiLocker PDF. Then calculate your exact percentage using our Best of 5 Calculator and check stream eligibility. If any marks look wrong, contact your school the same day — re-evaluation deadlines open quickly after results. See our complete Results Day Guide for the full checklist.
